What to expect from a session with me!

Not photogenic? No worries at all. I will direct you through your photo session and capture you at all your best angles. I'll guide you through simple prompts, hand and leg placings to keep the emotions as authentic as possible.

Styling

    Depending on the type of shoot, my clients are free to wear whatever they want. If you need help with styling, for example, what to wear for a couples session, I will give suggestions based on the vibe and location desired.

Location

    The great outdoors is my studio. I can give many suggestions on areas for nature, urban or historical themes.

Authenticity

    I believe this is the essential part of my job. My goal is to make my clients comfortable throughout the shoot, and when they receive their photos, I want them to recognize themselves. Recognize that their genuine emotions were captured and, most importantly, their natural beauty.
