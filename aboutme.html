How I got into photography:

    Photography has been fascinating to me since middle school when I saw people at school and family members purchase their first DSLRs. The ability to capture any expression and hold the photo physically in your hand or view it digitally showed me how precious life is. Photographing memories brings me so much joy, as we can reminisce on those memories, may they be good or bad. In 2015, I took a photography course in high school. My cousin let me borrow his Canon Rebel T2i throughout the year. After I was done with the course, he let me keep it. I was so excited to capture memories with a quality camera. I would bring that camera to all my cross country meets to photograph my teammates racing. At family functions, I was the designated photographer, capturing family and food. Fast forward to 2018, I began to reach out to my friends to model for me for fun, or I'd ask them if they'd want to catch up and photoshoot. After doing that multiple times, I grew my love for portrait photography.

What I can do for you:

    For every photo session I hold, my wish for my client is that they are comfortable and be as authentic as possible when I take their photo. Creative shoots are a different story when I'm directing for a specific mood or emotion, of course, but when someone asks me to take their photo, I want to capture the real them. The natural, raw, beautiful smiles that God has given them. I want my client to feel comfortable throughout the whole process and love the final product at the end.
